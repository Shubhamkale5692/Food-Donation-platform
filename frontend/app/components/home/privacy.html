Your Privacy Matters

At FoodBridge, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our platform to connect food donors with NGOs and Delivery Partners.

We believe in transparency and want you to understand exactly how your information is handled. By using FoodBridge, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

Data We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Personal Information: Name, email address, phone number, and profile details
  • Location Data: Pickup and delivery addresses for donations
  • Donation Details: Food type, quantity, and expiry information
  • Account Activity: Login history, donations made, and delivery status
  • Device Information: Browser type and IP address for security purposes

How We Use Your Data

Your data helps us provide and improve our services:

Connect Donors & NGOs

Match food donations with nearby NGOs

Manage Deliveries

Track and coordinate food pickups

Improve Platform

Analyze usage patterns for better service

Send Notifications

Updates about donations and deliveries
